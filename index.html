About Me

Paul is currently a Research Fellow and Software Developer at the Predictive Analytics Lab (PAL), where he works on LIMMMA (Landscapes integrative mapping and modelling for multifunctional analysis). His academic journey in Computer Science—earning both a BSc (2014-2017) and an MSc (2019-2020) from Liverpool Hope University—has laid the groundwork for a versatile career bridging both research and industry.

He previously contributed as a Research Assistant at the University of Huddersfield on projects like "Hansard at Huddersfield" and "HUM19" and gained practical experience as a Software Developer with LMS Recruitment Systems, along with a variety of freelance engagements.

His passion for machine learning is matched only by his curiosity about the ethical implications of AI. With aspirations to pursue a PhD in the near future, he continues to explore how innovative technology can be both powerful and responsible.
